Local Market: Initial sales point with lower profit margins.
Regional Market: Higher profit margins, requires more infrastructure.
Global Market: Highest profit margins, requires advanced logistics.

Machinery: Automate harvesting and packaging.

Weather Events: Affect banana production (e.g., storms, droughts).
Market Fluctuations: Changes in banana prices due to supply and demand.
Political Events: Trade tariffs, export restrictions, and subsidies
